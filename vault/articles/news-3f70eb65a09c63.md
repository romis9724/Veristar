# Watch: AND2BLE Takes 2nd Win For “Curious” On “Show Champion”; Performances By XLOV, ALPHA DRIVE ONE, And More

AND2BLE has won their second music show trophy for “Curious”!

On the June 3 episode of “Show Champion,” the candidates for first place were aespa&#8217;s &#8220;LEMONADE,&#8221; AND2BLE&#8217;s &#8220;Curious,&#8221; ALPHADRIVEONE&#8217;s &#8220;OMG!,&#8221; ILLIT&#8217;s &#8220;It’s Me,&#8221; and I.O.I&#8217;s &#8220;Suddenly.&#8221; AND2BLE ultimately took the win.
