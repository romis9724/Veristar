**The Black Label Inc.** (; stylized in all caps) is a South Korean record label and an associate company of YG Entertainment. It was founded in 2015 by YG producer Teddy Park and Kush.

## History
On September 22, 2015, YG Entertainment announced the creation of the independent sub-label to be headed by YG producers Teddy Park and Kush.<ref></ref> On March 17, 2016, Zion.T signed with the label.<ref></ref> On May 3, 2017, Okasian signed an exclusive contract with the label.<ref></ref> The label is also home to Jeon Somi, who signed with the label in September 2018, and debuted on June 13, 2019.<ref></ref> 

On November 16, 2020, as per the quarterly stock report of YG Entertainment, the label was converted to an associate company status.<ref></ref> On December 26, 2022, the company announced Taeyang joined the label but remained a member of Big Bang.<ref></ref> 

In January 2023, actor Park Bo-gum signed with the label.<ref></ref> In April 2023, The Black Label formed a joint venture with Thailand's largest conglomerate Charoen Pokphand Group called The Black Sea to expand its label into the Southeast Asian region.<ref name="Yang"></ref> 

On June 18, 2024, The Black Label announced that they had signed a management contract with Rosé from Blackpink for her solo endeavors.<ref></ref> On July 1, 2024, The Black Label announced the end of their exclusive contract with Zion.T.<ref></ref> On August 16, 2024, The Black Label announced the launch of their first girl group, Meovv. The group debuted on September 6, 2024.<ref></ref>
