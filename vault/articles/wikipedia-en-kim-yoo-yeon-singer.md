**Kim Yoo-yeon** (; born February 9, 2001), known mononymously as **Yooyeon**, is a South Korean singer and actress. She is a member of the girl group TripleS and its sub-units Acid Angel from Asia, Acid Eyes, EVOLution, Visionary Vision, Hatchi and Sun. She first garnered attention through her participation in the survival competition show *My Teenage Girl*.

## Early life
Kim Yoo-yeon was born on February 9, 2001,<ref></ref> in Banpo-dong, Seocho District, Seoul, South Korea. When she was young, she studied abroad in New Zealand and the United States to learn English.<ref name=abroad></ref> She attended Sehwa Girls' High School and attends Ewha Womans University majoring in Science Education as of 2024, but is on a leave of absence to pursue her dream of being a celebrity. She took the college entrance exam three times and passed the exam every time.<ref></ref> She previously was a student of Dongguk University in the department of Engineering but dropped out after the urging from her parents.<ref></ref> As she was growing up, her parents wanted her to pursue a career in the medical field due to her mother being a pharmacist and her father being a doctor. However, when attending university she wasn't sure if she was happy pursuing the university major that she chose and wanted to be a celebrity instead.<ref></ref> Initially, her parents were against her being a celebrity and expressed their disappointment in the idea.<ref></ref><ref name=auditon/>

## Career
### 2021–2022: *My Teenage Girl*

On September 8, 2021, Kim appeared in a trailer for *My Teenage Girl*, confirming her participation. At the beginning of the show, she was eliminated due to not having enough points to advance to the next round.<ref></ref><ref></ref> However, one of the show's mentors Jeon So-yeon revived her back into the show. Jeon stated, "when searching the term *My Teenage Girl* online, Kim shows up first on the searches. This shows that she is someone who can catch the eyes as a celebrity, and it is a charm that can be helpful to an idol group."<ref></ref> Kim consistently ranked within the top three as the show progressed, and unfortunately finished in eighth place in the final episode due to lack of benefit points, therefore being unable to debut in the final group.<ref name=MTG/>

###  2022–present: Debut with tripleS 
On July 11, 2022, Kim was announced to be a member of TripleS as the fifth member.<ref></ref> Kim stated that her 'heart had hurt' after seeing her fans continuously wait for her to make her debut, so she gave the idol life a second chance as she found herself to be the happiest when she is on stage.<ref></ref> Kim was personally offered to join TripleS by Jaden Jeong, the CEO of Modhaus, as he watched *My Teenage Girl*. She is typically known as an idol who had zero days of idol training.<ref name=auditon></ref> On September 16, 2022, TripleS announced they would be debuting their first two sub-units, with the members of the sub-units being voted by fans.<ref></ref> Kim was announced to be a member and the leader of the sub-unit Acid Angel from Asia on September 25, 2022, and the sub-unit officially debuted on October 27, 2022, with the debut extended play (EP) *Access* and "Generation" serving as the lead track.<ref></ref> Kim officially debuted in TripleS as 10 members on February 13, 2023, with the extended play *Assemble*.<ref></ref> On April 20, TripleS announced that they would be debuting their next sub-units.<ref></ref> On June 2, Kim became a member of the collaborative sub-unit, Acid Eyes, a collaboration of the two TripleS sub-units, Acid Angel from Asia and Krystal Eyes.<ref></ref> Kim was then announced as the first member and the leader of the sub-unit EVOLution on April 22 the same year.<ref></ref> The sub-unit debuted on October 11, 2023. In 2023, TripleS won several Rookie of the Year awards including MAMA Awards and Hanteo Music Awards. Kim made her acting debut on April 7, 2024, as the leading role in the web series *What's Up?*.<ref></ref> On June 18, Kim was announced to be placed into the sub-unit Visionary Vision.<ref></ref>

## Endorsements

In September 2022, Kim appeared as an actress in a GS25 commercial alongside fellow TripleS member Kim Chae-yeon.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In May 2023, she became the brand promoter for 'CODINGON X SeSAC'. She appeared as an actress in various commercials for CODINGON in 2023.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> In June 2023, Kim became a product packaging model for the Korean brand GRN+, for their product line 'Triple Festa'.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

## Public image and influence
### In the media
Since her participation in *My Teenage Girl*, Kim is often described as the "Goddess of Ewha Womans University",<ref name=visual/> "Goddess of *My Teenage Girl*",<ref></ref> "Eternal Goddess",<ref name=visual/> "Visual Center",<ref></ref> and "The Next Generation Ending Fairy".<ref name=fairy/> She was named one of the top five visual contestants of *My Teenage Girl*, along with fellow TripleS member Lee Ji-woo, as well as Classy members Myung Hyung-seo and Kim Ri-won, and Rescene's Minami.<ref name=visual></ref> She is often said to resemble DIA's Jung Chae-yeon and is said to share similar visuals, such as immaculate skin, delicate features, and an innocent image; netizens believed that their features look so much alike that they could be sisters.<ref></ref> Due to her ending fairy on *My Teenage Girl*, viewers said it gave a similar feel to Jung Chae-yeon's ending fairy during *Produce 101*.<ref name="fairy"></ref> At the time, she was also continuously ranked high in the most searched hot topic among non-drama television show topics.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

Since her debut in TripleS, Kim has been named as a "true growth character" as her singing and dancing skills were different and improved from before.<ref></ref> In June 2024, Kim entered the Girl group individual brand reputation results for the first time, resulting in 51st place.<ref></ref>

### Influence
Shortly after her reveal as a TripleS member, she gained attention online for her confident response to netizens concerns about her decision of taking absence from a prestigious university to become a K-pop idol.<ref></ref> When asked "isn't it a waste?", she confidently responded that she doesn't have any regrets, even if she dropped out she is confident enough that she can pass the CSAT again. She gained praise from netizens for her optimistic response.<ref></ref>

Park So-hyun, a member of TripleS and the producer of its sub-unit +(KR)ystal Eyes debut b-side track "Deja-vu" stated the lyric "니 꿈 속 안을 유영해" was written because she was thinking of Yoo-yeon. <ref></ref>

On June 28, 2024, Kim was chosen by *Forbes* Korea's Idol Pick for "Which model student celebrity would have been the student council president?". Due to this, she had a special article written in *Forbes* Korea July 2024 issue and was stated to be a role model to teenage fans.<ref></ref>
