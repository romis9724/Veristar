**Kim Young-kwang** (; born January 11, 1987) is a South Korean actor and model. Kim began his career as a model and has modeled for designers such as Alexander McQueen, Vivienne Westwood and Etro. In 2008, he was the first Asian model to model at Dior Homme's show.<ref></ref> As an actor, Kim has starred in the films *Hot Young Bloods* (2014) and *On Your Wedding Day* (2018), as well as the television dramas *Pinocchio* (2014), *The Guardians* (2017), *The Secret Life of My Secretary* (2019), *Call It Love* (2023), and the most recent series *Trigger* (2025).

## Early life
Born as Kim Young-kwang, Kim's father was a veteran of the Vietnam War and died when Kim was in elementary school.<ref></ref>

##  Career 
Kim began acting in 2008, and later performed in the drama *Good Doctor* on KBS2.<ref></ref> He also starred in the action comedy, *Runway Cop* (2012) and teen romance drama, *Hot Young Bloods* (2014).<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2014 he performed in dramas such as tvN's *Plus Nine Boys*<ref></ref> and SBS's *Pinocchio*,<ref></ref> for which he received an SBS Drama Award as "New Star" for his role as Seo Beom-jo.<ref></ref> In 2015, he starred in the medical drama *D-Day*,<ref></ref><ref></ref> and in the web drama *Dr. Ian* with Sandara Park.<ref></ref> In 2016, he was cast as the lead in the web drama *Gogh, The Starry Night* with Kwon Yuri,<ref></ref> and in the KBS2 romantic comedy series *Sweet Stranger and Me* with Soo Ae.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2017, he starred in the MBC thriller drama *The Guardians* as a passionate prosecutor.<ref></ref> In 2018, Kim starred in romance film *On Your Wedding Day* opposite Park Bo-young.<ref></ref> His performance in the film earned him a Best New Actor award in film at the Baeksang Arts Awards.<ref name="baek"></ref> He then starred in fantasy comedy film *The Soul-Mate* together with Ma Dong-seok.<ref></ref><ref></ref> Later that year, he was also cast in tvN's mystery drama *Room No. 9*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2019, Kim starred in the romance comedy drama *The Secret Life of My Secretary*, which premiered in May 2019.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

In 2021, he starred in the spy action film  *Mission: Possible* opposite Lee Sun-bin, which was released in February.<ref></ref> Also in 2021, he starred alongside Choi Kang-hee in the fantasy romantic comedy *Hello, Me!*, which premiered in February 2021. In 2022, he starred in murder thriller netflix series *Somebody*. In 2023, he starred in Melo-drama titled *Call It Love*, alongside actress Lee Sung-Kyung. It aired on Disney+ from February 22 to April 12, 2023. Also that year, he starred in the Genie TV original drama *Evilive*.

In 2025, he had a cameo appearance in *The Haunted Palace* as an *imoogi*.<ref name=":1"></ref> Followed by Netflix original series *Trigger*, which he starred alongside actor Kim Nam-gil.<ref name=":02"></ref> He also starred in a crime thriller series *Walking on Thin Ice*,<ref name=":03"></ref> also known as *Eunsoo's Good Day*, alongside actress Lee Young Ae. He also starred in the film adventure comedy film *The First Ride*.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
Kim maintains a close friendship with Lee Soo-hyuk, as well as other fellow models-turned-actors: Kim Woo-bin, Hong Jong-hyun and Sung Joon – with whom he has worked onscreen in *White Christmas*. They were given the nickname of "Model Avengers" by their fans.<ref></ref><ref></ref> On December 12, 2013, Kim began his mandatory military service. However, he was only required to be enlisted for six months as a public service worker due to his father being a Vietnam War veteran, as well as an acknowledged person of national merit. Kim was discharged in June 2014.<ref></ref>
