**Choi Hong-gi** (; February 11, 1951), known professionally as **Na Hoon-a** (), is a South Korean trot singer who debuted in 1966.<ref></ref>

##  Introduction 
Na Hoon-a is often referred to as the "Emperor of Trot" due to his charismatic personality and powerful voice.<ref></ref> He was one of the country's most popular singers of the 1970s, alongside his rival Nam Jin.<ref></ref> Na made international headlines in 2008 when he pulled down his pants on live television to dispel rumors that he had been castrated by a Japanese gangster.<ref></ref><ref name=":0"></ref> He is a legendary trot singer, and often appears on the KBS 1TV *Golden Oldies* ().<ref></ref>
