**Jung Suk-won** (; born May 16, 1985) is a South Korean actor.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> He began his entertainment career as a stuntman with the Seoul Action School after being discharged from the Marines, then turned to modeling and acting, notably in the television series *Rooftop Prince*,<ref></ref> and *Haeundae Lovers*.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

## Personal life
### Marriage and family
Jung began dating singer Baek Ji-young in 2011,<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> and the couple married on June 2, 2013, at the Sheraton Grande Walkerhill.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> On 22 May 2017, the couple's first child, a daughter named Jung Ha-im was born.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>
