**Shim Hye-jin** (; born February 6, 1966) is a South Korean actress and model. She was one of the leading actresses of the 1990s in South Korea. Shim started her career as a model and achieved stardom with a Coca-Cola commercial in the late 1980s. Due to her appearance in the commercial, Shim was dubbed "Cola-like Woman."<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref>(정경희) (2006.07.13)심혜진 VS 채시라 "드라마 시청률 대결"  The Chosun Ilbo</ref><ref>심혜진 "다 큰 딸 둔 아줌마 역, 어색하지 않았다" </ref><ref name="KMDb Korean"></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>
