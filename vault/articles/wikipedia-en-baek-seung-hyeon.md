**Baek Seung-hyeon** (born Baek Seung-wook on March 1, 1975) is a South Korean actor. Baek has starred in supporting roles in television series such as *Cain and Abel* (2009), *Brilliant Legacy* (2009), and *Prosecutor Princess* (2010).<ref></ref>
