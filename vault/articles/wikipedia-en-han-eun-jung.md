**Han Da-gam** (;<ref></ref> born July 10, 1980), formerly known as **Han Eun-jung** (), is a South Korean actress.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> She is known for *Successful Story of a Bright Girl* (2002), *Full House* (2004) and *Two Guys* (2004).<ref></ref>

## Personal life
Han married a businessman in 2020 after one year of dating. On April 28, 2026, Han announced her pregnancy.<ref></ref>
