**Yu-jin**, also spelled **Yoo-jin**, is a Korean given name. The name is popular as not only is it a traditional Korean name but it can be romanized as **Eugene**, allowing children to have not only a name that is Korean in origin but easy for Westerners to pronounce.<ref></ref> In the early 2000s, Yu-jin was among the most popular names for baby boys, but later in the decade, it saw a fall in popularity, and by 2008 Yu-jin had fallen out of the top ten.<ref></ref>
