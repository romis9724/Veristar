**Hong Jong-hyun** (born February 2, 1990) is a South Korean actor and model.<ref name=":0"></ref><ref></ref>

## Career
Hong began his entertainment career in 2007 as a professional model.<ref></ref> He made his acting debut in 2008,<ref name=one></ref>  and has appeared in the romantic comedy *Oh! My Lady* (2010), sitcom *Vampire Idol* (2011), followed by supporting roles in *Jeon Woo-chi* (2012) and *Dating Agency: Cyrano* (2013).<ref name=jeon></ref><ref name=dating></ref>
In 2014, Hong was cast in his first leading role in the cable series *Her Lovely Heels*,<ref name=heels></ref> followed by the melodrama *Mama*.<ref name=mama></ref> He also joined the fourth season of reality show *We Got Married*, pairing up with Girl's Day member Yura.<ref name=married></ref><ref></ref>

Hong next starred in the romantic comedy film *Enemies In-Law* and horror film *Alice: Boy from Wonderland* in 2015,<ref name=enemies></ref><ref name=alice></ref> followed by historical drama *Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo* in 2016.<ref name=moon></ref>

In 2017, he co-starred in historical melo-romance drama *The King in Love*.<ref name=king></ref>
In 2018, he was cast in the romantic comedy drama *My Absolute Boyfriend*, based on the Japanese manga series of the same name.<ref name=bf></ref> He is also set to star in the time-loop drama film *Spring, Again*.<ref name=spring></ref>

In March 2019, Hong is confirmed to star in the weekend drama *Mother of Mine*.<ref name=weekend></ref>

Hong enlisted for his mandatory service on December 2, 2019.<ref></ref>

After discharge from military service In October 2021, Hong attended the What We Lost Vol.2 photo exhibition as a photographer.<ref></ref>

In 2022, Hong returns to the original  TVING drama *Stock Struck*, after his discharge from the military.<ref></ref>

In September 2023, Hong signed with new agency Secret ENT.<ref></ref>
