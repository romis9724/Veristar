**Park Ji-yeon** (; born June 7, 1993), known mononymously as **Jiyeon**, is a South Korean singer, actress, model,  host and entertainer.<ref></ref> She debuted as a member of girl group T-ara in July 2009. Apart from her group's activities, she has also starred in various television dramas such as *Soul*, *Master of Study*, *Dream High 2*, *Triangle*, she also starred in various films such as *Death Bell 2: Bloody Camp*. She debuted as a solo artist with her first EP, *Never Ever*, on May 20, 2014, the first T-ara member to debut as a solo artist.

## Early life and education
Park was born in Seoul, South Korea, on June 7, 1993<ref name="Russell2014"></ref> to an ethnic Korean family.<ref> 티아라 지연, '악성루머'에도 '엠카'서 MC 활약 '눈길 . Naver. October 10, 2010. accessed November 14, 2015.</ref><ref> '엠카 MC 나선' 티아라 지연, 음란채팅 루머에도 '씩씩' " . *Naver News*. October 9, 2010. retrieved November 14, 2015.</ref> She briefly attended Hyehwa Girls' High School and graduated from Lila Art High School in 2012.<ref></ref> She didn't attend university to focus on her career.

## Career

### 2007–2009: Career beginning and T-ara
She and Hahm Eun-jung are the only two members of T-ara to be trained in acting. Instead, the two switched over to singing despite their original intentions.<ref>Lynn Kim & Jessica Kim: *"T-ara to become 7-member group" , *10Asia". July 16, 2010. retrieved July 26, 2010.</ref><ref> 티아라, 은정→보람 리더 교체 왜? ("Why did T-ara change its leaders from Eunjung to Boram?") . *Star News*. July 16, 2010. retrieved July 27, 2010.</ref>

Park has expressed an interest in modeling. In 2008, when she was 15 years old, she was featured in several pictorials and advertisements for clothing company Smart with Korean boy band SHINee.<ref> '음중' 새 4MC 온유·민호·지연·수지, 무난한 데뷔  . *Naver News*. October 30, 2010. retrieved November 4, 2010.</ref> She collaborated with Davichi and SeeYa on the digital single "Women's Generation", which was released in May 2009, making her the first member of T-ara to appear in public. She appeared in SG Wannabe's music videos "I Love You" and "Crybaby" from their 2009 album, *Gift from SG Wannabe*. Early on, the Korean media dubbed her as "Little Kim Tae-hee" due to her close resemblance to Kim.<ref>"티아라 지연, 김태희 '니트 머리띠' 열풍 동참". *Star News*. January 26, 2011. retrieved April 22, 2011.</ref> Park auditioned at the Mnet Casting System and joined the company in 2008. She subsequently debuted with T-ara on July 29, 2009.

In 2007, Park starred in *Lobbyist*. The following year, she starred in *Ae-ja and Min-ja*. On May 22, 2009, it was announced that Jiyeon would make her film debut with a supporting role in *Gas Station 2.*<ref></ref> However, on June 22, her label, CCM, confirmed that she had withdrawn from the project due to scheduling conflicts.<ref></ref>

In October 2010, Park became the host for MBC's *Show! Music Core*, alongside Bae Suzy, Choi Min-ho and Onew.<ref name=b></ref> She also became a cast member of SBS's variety show *Heroes*.<ref name=c></ref> In 2011, Park was cast in the Korean dub of the animated film, *Gnomeo and Juliet*.<ref name="a"></ref> In 2012, she was cast in the teen drama *Dream High 2*, starring Kang So-ra, Got7's JB and Jin-young, 2AM's Jinwoon, Sistar's Hyolyn, and Park Seo-joon.<ref name=":3"></ref><ref></ref>

### 2014–2017: Solo debut
Park made her solo debut in May 2014,<ref></ref><ref></ref> releasing the title track "Never Ever" (also known as "1 Minute 1 Second").<ref></ref><ref></ref> The music video ranked number one on China's largest music video platform YinYueTai for two weeks straight.<ref></ref> In June, Park was cast in MBC's television series *Triangle*, starring as the potential wife for Yim Si-wan's character.<ref name=":4"></ref> The same month, she began hosting SBS M's *The Show: All About K-pop* alongside Lee Hye-ri.<ref name="d"></ref>

In May 2015, Park was cast in the Korean-Chinese film *Encounter* alongside actor Lee Dong-gun. Due to unknown reasons, the film was never released.<ref name=":5"></ref><ref></ref> In July 2015, she collaborated with 2BiC's Jun-hyung on the song "Summer Love".<ref></ref> In 2016, Park was cast in the web drama *My Runway* alongside Kang Dong-ho.<ref name=":6"></ref> The drama was released on Netflix in December 2016.<ref></ref> In February 2017, it was announced that Park would be making her solo comeback after three years with a new album.<ref></ref> However, album preparations were later cancelled due to T-ara's controversy resurfacing.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

### 2018–present: Continued acting and *Senpass*
In early 2018, Park and her fellow T-ara members departed from MBK Entertainment following the expiration of their contracts. She later signed with Chinese agency Longzhen Culture Development in May 2018. Park and Vietnamese singer Soobin Hoàng Sơn have released music video for their collaboration track, "Between Us" exclusively on VLive, then on YouTube. However, the music video along with the audio video were deleted for unexplained reasons and were never uploaded again.<ref></ref> Jiyeon signed with Partners Park in November 2018 for her domestic activities.<ref></ref> On November 26, it was revealed that Park would release a digital single in mid December, as a thank you gift for fans. On December 22, 2018, Park released the music video for her winter ballad track "One Day".<ref></ref> On April 2, 2019, Park was cast in the KBS2's mystery, romantic comedy drama *I Wanna Hear Your Song* alongside Yeon Woo-jin, Kim Se-jeong and Song Jae-rim.<ref></ref> On December 26, 2019, Park released her second EP *SENPASS*, a portmanteau of "Sense" and "Compass". The EP consists of five-track including the title track "Take A Hike".<ref></ref>

On October 7, 2020, it was announced that actress Park will star in a new web drama called *Next Door Witch J*. She'll take the role of Seo Je Yi as a beauty creator.<ref></ref> In that same month of 2020, the news had been revealed that the actress will star another TV series called *Imitation* alongside Jung Ji-so, Lee Jun-young, Jeong Yun-ho which will air on KBS2 in the first half of 2021.<ref></ref> On July 12, 2021, Park left Partners Park after her contract expired.<ref></ref> On February 18, 2022, Park signed an exclusive contract with AnB Group.<ref></ref> On July 13, 2022, it was announced that Jiyeon will hold her first solo fan meeting, *RE:BLoom* at 7&nbsp;pm on the 30th at Yundang Art Hall.<ref></ref> On July 15, it was announced that Jiyeon would be releasing the 2022 version of the song "Rolling", which she sang 12 years ago for the OST of *God of Study*.<ref></ref>

##  Artistry 
Jiyeon is noted for a warm, sweet, and emotional vocal tone, particularly suited to ballads. Her vocals have been described as capable of "drawing listeners into the melodic and emotional atmosphere of a song".<ref></ref> Jiyeon's catalog spans various musical styles, including ballads, tango-influenced pop ("First Love"), OST tracks, and cross-national collaborations.<ref></ref> Her collaboration on the Vietnamese song "Đẹp nhất là em" (Between Us) showcased her ability to perform in Vietnamese with clear pronunciation.<ref></ref>

Her solo debut with *Never Ever* highlighted a more mature and sensual artistic image, combining singing, choreography, and stage performance.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

##  Impact 

###  Acting 
Park became one of the first girl group members of her generation to achieve both nationwide and international success, namely thanks to her acting projects, including *God of Study*, *Death Bell 2*, *Dream High 2*, and *Triangle*, most of which were licensed and exported overseas. In 2011, Chosun named her as the best acting-idol along with co-star Lee Joon following her first dubbing role in *Gnomeo and Juliet*.<ref></ref> In 2010, *Sports Seoul* ranked Jiyeon as the second best female idol-actress from expert perspective.<ref name=":8" /> In 2011, South Korean producer Kim Kwang Soo stated that Eun-jung and Ji-yeon were the most outstanding idol actors. He noted that although Jiyeon had not received formal acting training as a child, he recognized her potential to become a superstar. He added that casting her in *Soul* was a strong decision, as it allowed her to work alongside veteran actors and demonstrate her potential.<ref></ref>

###  Cultural impact 
Jiyeon frequently topped the most visited and popular Cyworld pages, a popular social platform at the time, since her debut with hundreds of thousands of weekly views.<ref></ref> In September 2011, plastic surgery clinic, Apgujeong Face Line, reported that Jiyeon has been placed first on their survey of the most wanted faces.<ref></ref> The same year, she was chosen as the most likable face in a survey conducted among 88 idols.<ref></ref> In 2012, Park was chosen again as the most popular female idol in a survey conducted by Regen Plastic Surgery.<ref></ref>

Park has been named as role model by several idols, including Kim Hye-in,<ref></ref> Rocket Punch,<ref name=":9"></ref> etc. In July 2021, Yeonhee and Yunkyung cited jiyeon as their role-models who inspired them to become idols after watching their performances in their hometown Gwangju.<ref name=":9"></ref> In 2017, *Forbes China* placed her at twenty-five at its Global Idol Chinese Popularity list.<ref name=":7" />

##  Endorsements 
Shortly after her debut as a singer/actress, Jiyeon was revealed to be a child model who has been active in the industry long before her debut. Jiyeon has modeled for various brands and shopping malls prior to her debut including SMART uniform advertisement for which she won the Model of the Year (Daesang) award in 2008, the commercial featured other k-idols including F(x)'s Victoria, Shinee's Taemin. Park also won the silver award at the APM Model Contest in 2007.<ref></ref>

In 2009, Jiyeon was chosen as cover model for the *Love Song* compilation album, a popular 2000s original soundtrack album which sold over 2,000,000 copies in South Korea alone. Jiyeon was chosen as model again the following year.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2010, Jiyeon was selected as a public relations ambassador of the National Pension Service by The Ministry of Health and Welfare. According to the national tax service the Ministry of Health and Welfare and affiliated organization, Jiyeon was paid a total of ₩55 million to advertise the service ranking, the highest among female celebrities.<ref></ref> In August 2016, Jiyeon worked as an advertising model with a cosmetic brand for about ₩360 million (₩30 million per month), the product was a personalized mask pack.<ref></ref> In 2024, Park, along with senior singer Uhm Jung-Hwa, have been selected as muses for "COMEONSTYLE", reportedly the largest shopping festival held by CJ ONSTYLE. The festival will be hosted by both singers in a form of an online show from April 5 to April 14 of the same year.<ref></ref>

In 2012, *The Voice of The People* Newspaper, reported that each of T-ara's member's individual advertising fee is around ₩400 million, one of the highest in the industry.<ref></ref>

##  Personal life 
In 2010, Jiyeon revealed that she had been training in Taekwondo since the age of five, with aspirations of becoming both a professional athlete and a Taekwondo instructor. She has also attained a third degree black belt.<ref></ref> Jiyeon has frequently demonstrated her Taekwondo abilities on live television programs such as *Haha Mong Show*, *World Changing Quiz*, and *Radio Star*, earning her the nickname "Strong Girl".<ref></ref>

On February 10, 2022, Park announced in a handwritten letter via personal SNS that she and baseball player Hwang Jae-gyun will get married in the winter of the same year.<ref></ref> They married in a private ceremony on December 10 at Shilla Hotel.<ref></ref> The ceremony was attended by the couple's closest family and friends, IU and Lee Hong-ki sang the congratulatory song.<ref></ref><ref></ref> According to *Chosun*, the couple's apartment located in Lotte World Tower, South Korea's tallest tower and 6th in the world, is worth ₩37B (approximately $28M), the most expensive reported celebrity house of all time in the country. A house-tour episode of the apartment aired on May 29 on tvN's *Doctor Freehan.*<ref></ref>

After months of speculation, on October 5, 2024, it was officially announced that after nearly two years of marriage, Park and Hwang had separated and mutually agreed to divorce. Through a statement released by Park's legal representative, it was confirmed that the couple had filed for divorce and come to the decision due to irreconcilable differences. Park also personally confirmed the news in a statement through her legal representative.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

##  Philanthropy 
In 2015, Jiyeon participated in "Arbor Day Campaign" along with Shannon, Speed, The SeeYa and Son Ho-jun.<ref></ref> In 2016, Park participated in the "Support Women Comfort" campaign. In remembrance of Sewol ferry disaster, all proceeds were donated to House of Sharing Foundation.<ref></ref>
