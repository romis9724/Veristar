BLACKPINK member Lisa has sparked heated reactions after offering fans a casual glimpse at her luxurious lifestyle.

Lisa | @lalalalisa_m/Instagram Recently, Lisa posted a series of pictures on her Instagram account, updating fans about her current whereabouts. The photos showed her cruising through the streets of Seoul, with a clear view of the Namsan Tower in the background. However, what drew the most attention was the idol’s insanely expensive vehicle.





In the photos, Lisa appeared to be driving a Ferrari Roma, a premium car model priced at around ₩380 million KRW (about $248,000 USD) to ₩400 million KRW (about $261,000 USD).

The picture immediately sparked a frenzy across social media, with fans wowing over Lisa’s young-and-rich lifestyle. Many were also reminded of the idol’s extravagant hobby of collecting luxury cars.
