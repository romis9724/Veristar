**Han Hye-jin** (; born October 27, 1981) is a South Korean actress. Han made her breakthrough in 2005 when she starred as a young widow in her 20s working as an outgoing hairdresser in the hit daily drama *Be Strong, Geum-soon!*.<ref></ref> Among her notable leading roles include Soseono in the historical epic *Jumong*,<ref></ref> Korea's first female doctor of Western medicine in *Jejungwon*,<ref></ref> and a sharpshooter in the manhwa film adaptation *26 Years*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> She also hosted the popular talk show *Healing Camp, Aren't You Happy* from 2011 to 2013.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
Han dated singer Naul (from R&B group Brown Eyed Soul) from 2003 to 2012.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

Han confirmed in March 2013 that she was dating South Korean midfielder Ki Sung-yueng,<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> and they announced their engagement two months later.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> Both are devout Christians.<ref></ref> The couple married on July 1, 2013, at the Hotel Intercontinental Seoul.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> They have a daughter, born on September 13, 2015.<ref></ref>
