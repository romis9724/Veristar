# Beloved Girl Group To Reunite After 12 Year Hiatus

**출처**: https://www.koreaboo.com/news/beloved-girl-group-secret-reunite-after-12-year-hiatus/
**피드**: Koreaboo

Beloved girl group Secret will reunite after a 12 year hiatus.

According to an exclusive report from Joy News24, Secret will be reuniting this year to begin the second chapter of the group. Original members Jeon Hyoseong and Jeong Hana (formerly known as Zinger) will recruit a third member to round out the trio.

The reunion project will be led by RBW and the group will present 2026 remake versions of their past hit songs. Such hits include &#8220;Magic&#8221;, &#8220;Shy Boy&#8221;, &#8220;Madonna&#8221;, and &#8220;Starlight Moonlight&#8221;.



RBW has already uplaoded a video of Hyoseong and Hana together on Secret&#8217;s new YouTube channel.
