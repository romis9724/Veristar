, known mononymously as **Rei** (; ), is a Japanese singer and rapper based in South Korea. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Ive, under Starship Entertainment.

## Early life
Rei was born on February 3, 2004, in Nagoya, Aichi, Japan.<ref></ref><ref name="rei"></ref>

In 2018, she joined Starship Entertainment after passing at the Loen Friends Global Audition in Japan where she performed Red Velvet's "Happiness." Rei's audition caught the judges' attention with her distinct voice. Later that year, she came to Korea and started training for approximately three and a half years.<ref></ref><ref></ref> During her training period, Rei attended the School of Performing Arts Seoul, where she was a student in the applied music department. She officially graduated on February 10, 2022.<ref></ref>

## Career
### 2021–present: Debut with Ive and solo activities

On November 6, 2021, Starship Entertainment revealed Rei as one of the six members of their new girl group, Ive. Rei was the fifth member to be introduced as the group's rapper and vocalist.<ref name="rei" /> On December 1, Rei officially debuted as a member of Ive with the release of their single album, *Eleven*, led by the single of the same name.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

On April 5, 2022, Ive released their second single album, *Love Dive*, on which Rei participated in writing the track titled "Royal".<ref name=":0"></ref> On August 22, Ive released their third single album, *After Like,* led by the lead single of the same name*,<ref></ref>* on which Rei is again credited as a writer.<ref name=":1"></ref> In 2023, she wrote four tracks for the group's first studio album, *I've Ive*, namely "Kitsch", "Hypnosis", "Not Your Girl", and "Next Page".<ref name=":2"></ref> In April 2023, she went on a hiatus from Ive activities to focus on her health.<ref></ref> Her condition improved and she returned at the end of May.<ref></ref>

In April 2025, Rei led Ive's viral "" (Pokju Ggini, Rampaging Guinea Pig) challenge from the chorus of "Attitude" off their third mini album *Ive Empathy*, where her initiating time-lagged arm-waving dance likened to hyperactive guinea pigs sparked a massive global frenzy on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts, earning her the nickname "" (Captain Ggini). Her spoiler videos amassed over 38.6 million TikTok views, solidifying her "all-time trendsetter" status following past viral hits like "" (Ribbon Dress) and "" (Kongsoon Pose).<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

On May 29, Rei served as a host at the Asia Star Entertainer Awards, marking her first time hosting a major awards ceremony.<ref></ref> On October 5, Rei attended the Valentino Spring/Summer 2026 fashion show during Paris Fashion Week alongside fellow Ive member Liz.<ref></ref> On October 15, Rei won the "Best Trend Leader" award at the TikTok Awards Korea 2025 held in Seoul. The award was reported to be related to her participation in a viral TikTok challenge promoting Ive's song "Attitude".<ref></ref><ref></ref> In November 2025, Rei released a cover of the song "Baby Powder", which was filmed in Paris during her European schedule, which included attending the Valentino Spring/Summer 2026 fashion show.<ref></ref>
