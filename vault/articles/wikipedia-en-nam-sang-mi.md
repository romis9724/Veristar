**Nam Sang-mi** (; born May 3, 1984) is a South Korean actress.

##  Career 
Nam was working in a Lotteria restaurant near Hanyang University in the fall of 2001 when someone posted her personal photos on the internet.<ref></ref> Her popularity as an ulzzang led to her discovery as an actress. She made her debut in the 2003 melodrama series *Love Letter*, and began her acting career by taking small roles in films, TV dramas, and commercials.<ref></ref> Her first leading role came with *Sweet Spy* in 2005.<ref></ref> This was followed by leading roles in comedy drama *Bad Family* (2006) and action romance drama *Time Between Dog and Wolf* (2007).<ref></ref>

In 2008, Nam starred as an aspiring food columnist in *Gourmet*, a hit series based on Huh Young-man's manhwa Sikgaek.<ref></ref>

Nam said regarding her role in horror film *Possessed* (2009), "The moment I read the script, I wanted this part badly. After such a long time away from films, it excited me, and I can show a completely different side than I've shown in dramas."<ref></ref> She next played the titular character in romantic comedy drama *Invincible Lee Pyung Kang*.<ref></ref>

After starring in close friend Ku Hye-sun's short film *You* (2010), Ku cast her as the leading lady of her second feature film *The Peach Tree* (2012).<ref></ref> Meanwhile, on the small screen, Nam starred in the retro music drama *Lights and Shadows* (2011)<ref></ref> and romantic comedy drama *Goddess of Marriage* (2013).<ref></ref>

In 2014, Nam reunited with *Time Between Dog and Wolf* costar Lee Joon-gi in the period drama *Gunman in Joseon*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> This was followed by a starring role in the comedy film *Slow Video*.<ref></ref>

In 2017, Nam starred in the office comedy drama *Good Manager*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2018, Nam starred in the mystery thriller series *Let Me Introduce Her*.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
### Marriage and family
Nam married a businessman at a small church in Yangpyeong County, Gyeonggi Province on January 24, 2015.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> She gave birth to a baby girl on the afternoon of November 12, 2015.<ref></ref><ref></ref>
