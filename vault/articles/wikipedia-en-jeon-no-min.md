**Jeon No-min** (born Jun Jae-yong on August 28, 1966) is a South Korean actor. Among his recent roles were the villainous Hwarang warrior Seolwon in *Queen Seondeok*,<ref></ref> and the fictional independence fighter Damsari in *Bridal Mask*.
