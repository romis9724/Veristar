**Jang Jin-young** (), known professionally as **Jang Da-ah** (; born May 5, 2001), is a South Korean actress and model under King Kong by Starship.<ref></ref><ref></ref> She is best known for her role as Baek Ha-rin in *Pyramid Game*.

##  Early life 
Jang Da-ah was born in Ichon-dong, Seoul, South Korea, on May 5, 2001.<ref name="mbn"></ref> She has a younger sister, Jang Won-young, who is a member of the South Korean girl group Ive.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

##  Career 
On April 7, 2023, Jang signed with King Kong by Starship.<ref></ref><ref name="mbn" /> The report stated that she would debut as an actress and go by the stage name Jang Da-ah.<ref></ref> Her profile was revealed along with her first appearance in public as a model for Acuvue.<ref></ref><ref name="acuvue"></ref> On May 26, the agency confirmed that Jang would make her acting debut in the TVING original drama *Pyramid Game* (2024) based on the Naver webtoon of the same name.<ref></ref> The series aired in February 2024 with Jang playing the lead role of Baek Ha-rin, a mysterious and popular student whose elegant appearance belied a sociopathic personality.<ref name=":0"></ref><ref name="extra"></ref><ref></ref> Jang came to international attention for her role, which was positively received by critics, with *NME* praising Jang for her authentic characterization of the cold-blooded, narcissistic Baek Ha-rin.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> For her performance, she was nominated for Best New Actress at the 3rd Blue Dragon Series Awards and the 10th APAN Star Awards, and won Rookie of the Year at the 9th Asia Artist Awards.<ref name="BlueDragon2024" /><ref name="APAN2024" /><ref name="AAA2024 "/> In 2025, Jang starred in the ENA series *My Troublesome Star* as the young version of Uhm Jung-hwa's character, Im Se-ra.<ref></ref> In 2026, Jang made her film debut with the horror film *Salmokji: Whispering Water*, playing Moon Se-jeong, a young PD who runs a horror vlog channel.<ref name="Salmokji" />

##  Endorsements 
In April 2023, Jang made her modeling debut in an advertisement for Acuvue.<ref name="acuvue"/> In May, she became the new muse of beauty brand Suiskin.<ref></ref> In May 2025, Jang modeled for South Korean clothing brand Kenneth Lady.<ref></ref> As a model, Jang has been featured in publications such as *Cine21*, *Allure Korea*, *Men Noblesse*, and *Singles Korea*.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>
