***Sold Out on You*** () is a 2026 South Korean romantic comedy workplace television series written by Jin Seung-hee, directed by Ahn Jong-yeon, and starring Ahn Hyo-seop, Chae Won-bin, Kim Bum, and Go Doo-shim. The series depicts the story of two people who are sincere and passionate about their work but surprisingly neglectful of themselves, as their lives become intertwined 24 hours a day. It aired on SBS TV from April 22, to May 28, 2026, every Wednesday and Thursday at 21:00 (KST). It is also available for streaming on Netflix.

##  Synopsis 
Matthew Lee and Dam Ye-jin meet through a series of day-and-night encounters. As their paths continue to cross, they slowly begin to understand each other's struggles, from his hidden burdens to her battle with insomnia. Through their growing connection, they help one another face personal challenges, heal emotional wounds, and discover love amid the chaos of everyday life.

##  Cast and characters 
###  Main 
* Ahn Hyo-seop as Matthew Lee / Lee Hae-seok
: Co-CEO of Gojeuneok Bio, a natural raw materials company, and a cosmetics researcher known for his technical expertise. Living under the alias Matthew Lee to distance himself from his past as Lee Hae-seok, he operates a large-scale mushroom farm in a rural village, where he is the sole producer of the "white-flowered uri mushroom". Despite his attempts to remain detached, he is an active member of the local community, where he is nicknamed "Mechuri". His life is disrupted when he terminates a contract with the corporation L'Étoile, making him the target of a persistent Ye-jin.<ref></ref>
* Chae Won-bin as Dam Ye-jin
: A show host at Hit Homeshopping. Following a controversial debut five years prior, she maintains a work-centric lifestyle and suffers from insomnia. After losing her prime-time slot to a rival, she is tasked with securing an exclusive contract with L'Étoile. This mission leads her to Deokpung Village to negotiate with Matthew.<ref></ref>
* Kim Bum as Seo Eric / Eric Desruets
: Executive Director of L'Étoile, a global skin care brand, and heir to the Desruets family. After years of traveling to avoid family succession conflicts, he takes a leadership role in the company. He seeks to reconnect with Ye-jin, whom he met in Korea three years ago, using a contract dispute with Gojeuneok Bio as a pretext to return to the country.<ref></ref>

##  Production 
###  Development 
The series is directed by Ahn Jong-yeon, who helmed *Seoul Busters* (2024), with the script written by rookie writer Jin Seung-hee. Production is handled by , the company behind *Nevertheless* (2021), *The Killer's Shopping List* (2022), *Somebody* (2022), and *The Scandal of Chunhwa* (2025), along with Studio S and Slingshot Studios.<ref name="IZE" /><ref name="SN" />

It was first given the working title *Some&Shopping* (). Development phase started in October 2023, but due to long delays, it barely managed to move forward in March 2025 under the new title.<ref name="newsen"></ref>

###  Casting 
In October 2023, the male leading role was offered to Jung Hae-in, however, he eventually declined.<ref name="newsen" />

In March 2025, Ahn Hyo-seop and Chae Won-bin were cast as lead of the series.<ref name="IZE"></ref><ref></ref> Two months later, Ahn and Chae were confirmed to star.<ref></ref> Kim Bum along with Go Doo-shim, Park Ah-in, Shin Dong-mi, Jo Woo-ri, and Woo Hee-jin also joined the cast a day after the confirmation of the lead actors.<ref></ref> In July 2025, Yoon Jae-chan has been cast.<ref name="SN"></ref> Other supporting cast includes , Kim Seo-an, Jo Bok-rae, and Park Ye-young.<ref>Support casting:
