# aespa’s Karina Caught Up In Another Political Controversy

**출처**: https://www.koreaboo.com/news/aespa-karina-caught-political-controversy-instagram/
**피드**: Koreaboo

On June 2, aespa’s Karina posted a series of photos on Instagram that garnered much attention for the color of her outfit.

In the photos, she is wearing a bright blue sleeveless top, blue contact lenses, and a checkered skirt.



However, some netizens speculated that posting photos wearing a specific color just one day before the nationwide local elections was intentional.



Other netizens defended Karina, stating that it was just a stage outfit and that it wasn’t posted on actual election day.

Previously, Karina was embroiled in a controversy over posting a photo of herself wearing a red jacket with the number two on it during last year’s Presidential election. The controversy grew so large that even political figures discussed her post, which she later deleted.
