**Kwon Hyuk** is a South Korean actor and model.<ref></ref> He is known for his roles in dramas such as *Top Star U-back*, *Graceful Friends* and *A Good Supper*.<ref></ref> He also appeared in the movie *The Point Men*.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

## Early life
He was born on February 19, 1989, in South Korea.<ref></ref> 

## Career
In 2018 he joined Studio & News, he first did modeling for various promotional videos of Hyundai Sonata N Line, Samsung QLED, Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance, Korea Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, SMEG Brand Film and KDB Development Bank, KT Membership Shopping Discount, KT Galaxy Note 9, HUG Housing City Fund.<ref></ref> The very same year he made his debut as an actor in drama *Top Star U-back* and appeared in music video "Winter-Somebody's tale".<ref></ref> In 2020 he appeared in drama *Graceful Friends* also starring in the movie *The Point Men*.<ref></ref> In 2021, he landed his first main role in a television series in the MBC drama *A Good Supper*, appearing alongside Jae Hee, Jung Woo-yeon and Kang Da-hyun.<ref></ref>
