# K-Pop Boy Group Brutally Friend-Zones Their Fandom

**출처**: https://www.koreaboo.com/stories/kpop-boy-group-friendzone-fans-fandom/
**피드**: Koreaboo

K-Pop and parasocial relationships go hand in hand, and some agencies have even marketed their groups with a boyfriend or girlfriend concept.

BOYFRIEND One of these groups is BOYFRIEND, a boy group formed by Starship Entertainment in 2011. In 2019, BOYFRIEND disbanded, but they reformed in 2021 and returned with the digital single &#8220;Ending Credit&#8221;. That same year, they made a comeback under the new name BF, but they&#8217;re now back to promoting under their original name.
