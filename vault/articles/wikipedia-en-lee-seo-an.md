**Lee Soo-mi** (; born March 3, 1989), professionally known by her stage name **Lee Seo-an**, is a South Korean actress and singer. Using her birth name, she debuted as a new member of SeeYa in 2009 and active for short-time prior being included in a new group line-up Coed School in 2010 by Core Contents Media and later debuted with the girl group F-ve Dolls in 2011. In 2012, Lee terminated her contracts with Core Contents Media and signed D-Business Entertainment in 2013 to pursue her solo career. After taking a break for three-years, Lee transitioned into acting appearing on China IQIYI's *Teacher Good Night* in 2016 under the stage name Lee Seo-an.

##  Early life 
Lee auditioned for "SBS's Young Jae Yook Sung Project" in 2001 and won but never became a trainee.<ref></ref> She attended the Korea Aerospace University and graduated in Aeronautical Science & Flight Operations.

##  Career 

### 2009–2012: Career beginnings
In August 2009, Lee was announced as a new member of SeeYa to replace Nam Gyu-ri who left the group.<ref></ref> Lee officially debuted with the release of the group's mini album *Rebloom* on October 28.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/117/0002043626</ref> 

On July 23, 2010, it was announced that Lee would join the line-up of a new Co-ed group, Coed School, which also confirmed that she was dropped out from Seeya.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/108/0002040441</ref> The group officially debuted with released mini album *Something That Is Cheerful and Fresh* on September 27, 2010.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/108/0002049008</ref> In December 2010, Core Contents announced the formation of Coed School's female unit called 5Dolls (later F-ve Dolls), including Lee and other girl members of Coed School and a new member.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/108/0002062525</ref>
On February 16, 2011, the group released their debut mini album *Charming Five Girls*.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/111/0000222846</ref>

In February 2012, Lee left the group to pursue her solo career.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/108/0002127156</ref>

### 2013–present: Transition into acting
In December 2013, Lee was announced to returns as actress and would starred in D-Unit new song "It's You" music video.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/108/0002265343</ref>

Lee were cast in China drama IQIYI's *Hello Mr.Right* in 2016.<ref>https://m.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201611180637053210&r=1</ref>

In July 2019, Lee was cast as Jung Hae-jin in KBS2 drama *Justice*.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/382/0000743548</ref>

In August 2020, Lee confirmed to cast as Oh Young-ju in KBS's *Do Do Sol Sol La La Sol*.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/109/0004258690</ref>

In November 2021, Lee starred in JTBC's drama *The Spy City* as Chaebol youngest daughter named Jung Eun-jung.<ref>https://m.entertain.naver.com/home/article/112/0003502398</ref>
