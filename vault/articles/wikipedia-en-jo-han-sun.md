**Jo Han-sun** (; born June 17, 1981) is a South Korean actor. He made his debut in a beer commercial in 2001, then became a household name through the sitcom *Nonstop 3* (2002) and the television drama *April Kiss* (2004). He is best known for the films *Temptation of Wolves* (2004), *Cruel Winter Blues* (2006), and *A Better Tomorrow*, the 2010 remake of the Hong Kong classic.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref>Lee, Jong-gil (9 September 2010).
