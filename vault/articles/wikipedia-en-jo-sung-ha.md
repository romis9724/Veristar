**Jo Sung-ha** (; born August 8, 1966) is a South Korean actor. He is best known for his supporting roles in *The Yellow Sea* (2010), *Helpless* (2012), and *Korean Peninsula* (2012)<ref></ref><ref></ref> and *The K2* (2016).
