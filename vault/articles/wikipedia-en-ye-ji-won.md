**Ye Ji-won** (; born February 1, 1973) is a South Korean actress.

## Career
 in 2016]]
She is best known for her leading role in the hit sitcom *Old Miss Diary*,<ref></ref><ref></ref> and her more serious turns in Hong Sang-soo films *Turning Gate* and *Hahaha*.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

In October 2018, Ye signed with new agency The Queen AMC.<ref></ref>
