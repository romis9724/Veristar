**Hong Soo-hyun** (; born February 15, 1981) is a South Korean actress. She is best known for her roles in *Dae Jo-yeong*, *The Princess' Man*, and *Goodbye Dear Wife*.

##  Career 
After she debuted as a magazine model in high school, Hong appeared in a Johnson & Johnson commercial in 1996, and began her acting career in 1999. In the following decade, true stardom remained elusive as she mostly found herself restricted to supporting roles.<ref name="sun"></ref> Then Hong received strong reviews for her back-to-back strong performances in the 2011 period drama *The Princess' Man* as a role Princess Kyunghye, and the 2012 quirky comedy *History of a Salaryman*,<ref name="sun" /><ref></ref> as well as *Goodbye Dear Wife* (2012).

##  Personal life 
### Marriage
On May 28, 2021, Hong married her non-celebrity boyfriend in a private wedding ceremony.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

###  Philanthropy 
On August 9, 2022, Hong donated  to help those affected by the 2022 South Korean floods through the Hope Bridge Korea Disaster Relief Association.<ref></ref>
