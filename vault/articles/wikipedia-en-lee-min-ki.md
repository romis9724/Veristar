**Lee Min-ki** (; born January 16, 1985) is a South Korean actor, singer, and model. Lee's first leading role in television was in the 2005 sports drama *Taereung National Village*. He has gone on to star in many television series, including *Love Truly* (2006), *Dal-ja's Spring* (2007), * Because This Is My First Life* (2017), *The Beauty Inside* (2018), *My Liberation Notes* (2022) and *Behind Your Touch* (2023). Lee has also starred in feature films, most notably in the box office hits *Tidal Wave* (2009), *Quick* (2011), *Spellbound* (2011) and *Very Ordinary Couple* (2013).

## Career

### Acting
After honing his acting in one-act dramas, Lee Min-ki first gained popularity on television in romantic comedies *Love Truly* (2006) and *Dal-ja's Spring* (2007).<ref name=spring></ref><ref name=truly></ref> He then starred in the quirky low-rated cult series *Evasive Inquiry Agency* (2007)<ref name=gold></ref> and alternated mainstream films with indies such as *Oishii Man* (2009).<ref name=man></ref><ref></ref> He gained favorable notices for his supporting roles in the thriller *A Million* as a hot-tempered Marine-turned-janitor who finds himself involved in a survival reality show,<ref name=million></ref> and the disaster film *Tidal Wave*, in which he plays a lifeguard who makes a heartbreaking sacrifice when a tsunami hits the Korean beach town of Haeundae.<ref name="acting"></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>
Lee then solidified his bona fide movie star status with his leading roles in the action film *Quick* and romantic comedy *Spellbound*.<ref name=quick></ref><ref></ref><ref name=spell></ref>

Lee starred alongside Kim Min-hee in the relationship drama *Very Ordinary Couple* (2013), a critically-acclaimed a box-office hit.<ref name=couple></ref><ref></ref> 
He followed with leading roles in thriller *Monster* (2014),<ref></ref><ref name=monster /> action noir *For the Emperor* (2014),<ref name=emperor></ref> and drama film *Shoot Me in the Heart* (2015), based on the bestselling novel of the same name by Jeong Yu-jeong.<ref></ref><ref name=shoot></ref>

In 2017, Lee starred in the romantic-comedy drama *Because This Is My First Life* opposite Jung So-min, which had been his first television leading role in 10 years.<ref name=life></ref> This was followed  with the fantasy melodrama *The Beauty Inside* (2018), based on the 2015 romantic comedy film of the same name. He played a man who suffers from prosopagnosia.<ref name=beauty></ref>

In 2019, Lee returned to the small screen opposite Lee Yoo-young in the crime detective drama *The Lies Within* as detective Jo Tae-sik.<ref name=Lies></ref> On January 13, 2020, it was announced that Lee would star in the occult thriller film *Three Days* alongside Park Shin-yang and Lee Re.<ref name="entertain.naver.com"></ref>

In 2021, Lee starred alongside Nana in the MBC romantic comedy *Oh My Ladylord*.<ref name="n.news.naver.com"></ref> After the contract with his former agency expired in September 2021, Lee signed a contract with Yeongsang ENT.<ref></ref> The same year, he joined the JTBC television series *My Liberation Notes* which premiered in April 2022. He played the role of Yeom Chang-hee, the second of three siblings with no proper goal in life.<ref></ref>

In 2026, Lee starred in dark comedy short film *Grim Reaper Life Extension Project* as Kang-rim, a senior grim reaper who always deviates from the rules but ranks first in performance, alongside Monsta X's Hyungwon.<ref name="grlep" />

### Singing
After performing a stylized version of "My Way" at the 2008 Mnet KM Music Festival, Lee lent his vocals to a track with Korean electronica/pop singer Sheean in a collaboration with Japanese electronica/Shibuya-kei artist FreeTEMPO. The song, titled "Power of Love," was released as a digital single, and Lee also appeared in the music video.<ref></ref> 
On August 11, 2009, he unveiled his musical debut *No Kidding*, a six track EP with a very indie-electro rock sound.<ref></ref>  The album's title song "Eternal Summer" was the first and only single released.<ref></ref> 
In 2013, he released two singles titled "Those Days I Had with You" and "Everything" in collaboration with Gold Coast.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
### Military service
Lee enlisted for his mandatory military service on August 7, 2014, and was assigned as a public service worker.<ref></ref><ref></ref> He was discharged in August 2016.<ref></ref>
