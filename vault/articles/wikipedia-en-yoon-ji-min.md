**Yoon Ji-young** (; born December 9, 1977<ref name="Jimin"/>), known professionally as **Yoon Ji-min** (), is a South Korean actress and model. She played Ji in the period action series *Warrior Baek Dong-soo*.

## Personal life
She married actor Kwon Min (Kwon Hae-sung) on July 13, 2013 at THE RAUM, Seoul.<ref></ref>  On December 20, 2014, Ji-min gave birth to her daughter.<ref></ref>
