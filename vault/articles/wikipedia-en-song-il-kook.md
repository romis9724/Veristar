**Song Il-kook** (; born 1 October 1971<ref name="naver"/>) is a South Korean actor. He is best known for his role in the 2006 hit series *Jumong* as the titular character.

## Early life and education
Song is the grandson of politician Kim Du-han, great grandson of Kim Chwa-jin, the famous anarchist and Korean independence movement general during the early 1900s. Song is the son of politician and former actress Kim Eul-dong.<ref></ref>

He graduated from Cheongju University, majoring in performing arts, and later obtained a master's degree from Chung-Ang University.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

## Personal life
Song is the vice-president of the Korean Triathlon Confederation<ref></ref><ref></ref> and participated in the 2008 Seoul International Triathlon Competition.<ref></ref> In April 2008, Song carried the Olympic torch through Seoul.<ref name="naver"></ref><ref></ref>

Song is also an amateur sketch artist.<ref></ref> He has also modelled professionally, both on the runway and in print. In 2008, he was one of the subjects of the Kolon Christmas Photo Shoot.<ref></ref>

In 2009, the state of Hawaii designated March 21 as Song Il-kook day.<ref></ref> In April 2010, he was invited to the Blue House to dine with Heads of State, including the president of Kazakhstan, as *Jumong* garnered immense popularity in the central Asian country.<ref></ref>

### Marriage
On 15 March 2008, Song was wed to High Court Judge Jung Seung-yeon in a private, traditional Korean wedding at the Sheraton Walkerhill Hotel in Seoul.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

Song and Jung became parents to fraternal triplet sons at a hospital in Seoul on 16 March 2012.<ref></ref> Their sons are named Dae-han, Min-guk and Man-se which, when said together mean "Long live the Republic of Korea". Song and his sons starred in the variety show *The Return of Superman* from 6 July 2014 to 7 February 2016. The triplets also made a cameo appearance in Song's television series *Jang Yeong-sil* (2016).

Song adheres to a pescetarian diet for both health and ethical reasons.<ref>Lee, Seo-hyun, '송일국-이하늬-윤진서-김효진-배종옥' 공통점은!
