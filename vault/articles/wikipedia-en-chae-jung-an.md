**Chae Jung-an** (born Jang Jung-an on October 21, 1977) is a South Korean actress and singer.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

##  Philanthropy 

On March 11, 2022, Chae made a donation of  million to the Hope Bridge Disaster Relief Association to help the victims of the massive wildfire that started in Uljin, Gyeongbuk and has spread to Samcheok, Gangwon.<ref></ref>

In May 2022, Chae donated  million to the Green Umbrella Children's Foundation with 'Genetic', a beauty brand specializing in aesthetics.<ref></ref>
