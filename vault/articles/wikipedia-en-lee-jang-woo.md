**Lee Jang-woo** (; born June 1, 1986) is a South Korean actor and singer. He is best known for his roles in the television series *Glory Jane*, *I Do, I Do*, *My Only One* and  *Homemade Love Story*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> Lee also became popular after being paired with Hahm Eun-jung in the third season of fictional marriage reality show *We Got Married*.

In 2009, Lee, No Min-woo, and Hyun Woo formed the K-pop project group 24/7, which has since disbanded after releasing the single "24 Hours a Day, 7 Days a Week". Lee also appeared in a 2011 music video for his cousin, singer Hwanhee.<ref></ref>

##  Personal life 
On June 22, 2023, it was confirmed that Lee was in a relationship with actress Jo Hye-won.<ref></ref> On June 21, 2025, Hunus Entertainment confirmed that the couple would be getting married in November.<ref></ref> Lee and Jo married on November 23, 2025.<ref></ref>
