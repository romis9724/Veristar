**Geum Bo-ra** (; born February 21, 1961), birth name **Son Mi-ja** (), is a South Korean actress. She made her acting debut in 1979, and won Best New Actress at the 1980 Grand Bell Awards for *Water Spray*.<ref></ref> She was active in Korean cinema in the 1980s, and when she grew older, switched to supporting roles in television.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

Geum married businessman Oh Jae-hee in 1989, but financial problems led to their divorce in 2002. They have three sons: former Yonsei University basketball player Oh Seung-jun, Oh Seung-min, and a third son.<ref></ref> She remarried in November 2005 to widowed businessman Kim Seong-taek, and one of her two stepdaughters is Kim Hyeon-jin.<ref></ref><ref></ref>
