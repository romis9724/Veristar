**Lim Ju-eun** (; born January 7, 1988) is a South Korean actress.

## Career
Lim is best known her roles in the horror thriller *Soul* (also known as *Hon*),<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> the campus musical drama *What's Up*,<ref></ref> and the quirky rom-com *Wild Romance*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2014, she costarred in the web series One Sunny Day with So Ji-Sub.

In September 2018, Lim signed with new agency Hunus Entertainment.<ref></ref>

In November 2022, Lim left Hunus Entertainment and signed with a new agency SidusHQ.<ref></ref>
