**Chun Jung-myung** (, born November 29, 1980) is a South Korean actor. Chun made his break into the entertainment industry in the KBS teen drama *School 2* (1999). He is best known for his leading roles in TV dramas such as *Fashion 70's* (2005),<ref></ref> *Goodbye Solo* (2006),<ref></ref> *What's Up Fox?* (2006),<ref></ref> *Cinderella's Sister* (2010),<ref></ref><ref></ref> *The Duo*,<ref></ref><ref></ref> *Glory Jane* (2011),<ref></ref><ref></ref> *Reset* (2014),<ref></ref><ref></ref> *Heart to Heart* (2015),<ref></ref> and *The Master of Revenge* (2016).<ref></ref>

He also starred in the films *The Aggressives* (2005),<ref></ref><ref></ref> *Les Formidables* (2006), *Hansel and Gretel* (2007),<ref></ref><ref></ref> and *Queen of the Night* (2013).<ref></ref>

Chun started his own management company in 2011.<ref></ref> Since the early 2010's, Chun has trained Brazilian Jiu Jitsu and is a purple belt under John Frankl.<ref></ref> In his Instagram post last June 2022, the actor shared that he has attained the black belt in Jiu Jitsu, after 11 years of hardwork.<ref></ref>
