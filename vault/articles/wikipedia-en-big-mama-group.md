**Big Mama** () is a South Korean female group that debuted in 2003.<ref></ref> The group focused on its members' singing abilities rather than their looks.<ref></ref> The group went on hiatus in 2012 after their song "Cleaning My Closet".<ref></ref> The group reunited on June 24, 2021 with their digital single "One More Day", ending their 9 year hiatus.

As of 2026, the group has currently released six studio albums, two cover albums and 18 singles.

##  Members 
* Shin Yona (신연아, 申然雅)
* Lee Ji-young (이지영)
* Lee Young-hyun (이영현)
* Park Min-hye (박민혜)
