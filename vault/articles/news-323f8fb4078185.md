# Insiders Spill The Details Between K-Drama Actress And Top Singer’s Breakup Rumors

**출처**: https://www.koreaboo.com/news/insiders-spill-details-k-drama-actress-top-singers-breakup-rumors/
**피드**: Koreaboo

Jannabi&#8216;s Choi Jung Hoon and actress Han Ji Min are reportedly still happily together despite recent breakup rumors that spread online.

According to a report released on June 4, the celebrity couple continues to maintain a strong relationship and was even recently spotted enjoying a date together in Seoul.

Sources claimed that Choi Jung Hoon and Han Ji Min spent time together at the end of May, with witnesses describing them as looking just like any other affectionate couple. The two reportedly did not attempt to hide their relationship and were said to have warmly greeted people who recognized them, even signing autographs.



The report comes shortly after Choi Jung Hoon became the subject of unexpected breakup speculation following an emotional appearance at a university festival.

Recently, a video titled &#8220;Jannabi&#8217;s Choi Jung Hoon Crying At Konkuk University&#8217;s Festival&#8221; went viral online. During the performance, the singer became emotional and shed tears on stage. After clips of the moment spread online, some netizens began speculating that the tears may have been related to his personal life, with rumors emerging that he and Han Ji Min had broken up.

The speculation quickly gained traction despite the lack of any evidence connecting his emotional performance to his relationship.
