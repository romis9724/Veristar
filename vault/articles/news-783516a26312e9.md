# Popular Idol Responds To Viral Criticism Calling Her New Song The “Worst Ever”

AleXa (also known as Kim Seri) has responded to criticism over her upcoming release.

AleXa | Instagram AleXa, known to many for her time on Produce 48 and for winning the American Song Contest, left her agency last year, beginning a new journey as an artist. Her first independent release, &#8220;Vital Check,&#8221; was released in April 2025, and her track &#8220;Tongue Tied&#8221; was released for the Swedish song contest Melodifestivalen 2026 in February 2026.



Recently, the idol has begun teasing her next release, titled &#8220;UMAMI,&#8221; including a peek at the choreography.
