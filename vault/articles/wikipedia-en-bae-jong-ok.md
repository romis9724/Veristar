**Bae Jong-ok** (; born May 13, 1964) is a South Korean actress.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> She debuted as a TV actress after she was recruited by KBS,<ref name="KMDb Korean"></ref> and has since been active in theater, film and television.<ref></ref>

While concurrently maintaining an acting career, Bae completed a doctorate at Korea University. Her thesis was on the correlation between production crews and reactions of netizens.<ref></ref> She has taught Theater and Film Studies at Chung-Ang University as a visiting professor since 2003.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
Bae married a pilot in 1994, but the couple divorced in 1996. She has a daughter studying in the United States.<ref></ref>

Bae adheres to a pescatarian diet.<ref>Lee, Seo-hyun (4 January 2012). '송일국-이하늬-윤진서-김효진-배종옥' 공통점은!  [Song Il-gook, Lee Han-ui, Yoon Jin-seo, Kim Hyo-jin and Bae Jong-ok have something in common] (in Korean). Shinmoongo.</ref>
