**Son Chae-young** (; born April 23, 1999), known mononymously as **Chaeyoung**, is a South Korean singer and rapper. She is a member of the girl group Twice, formed by JYP Entertainment in 2015.

Chaeyoung released her debut studio album *Lil Fantasy Vol. 1* and its lead single "Shoot (Firecracker)" in September 2025.

## Life and career
### Early life and pre-debut activities
Chaeyoung was born in Seoul, South Korea on April 23, 1999.<ref name="ChaeOfTwice" /><ref></ref> She took interest in the performing arts from a young age and used to model for a children's magazine.<ref name="ChaeOfTwice" /> Chaeyoung decided she wanted to become a singer before joining JYP Entertainment and took dance lessons for over one year.<ref name="ChaeOfTwice" /> At age 13, she passed the auditions and ended up joining the company. As a trainee, she appeared in the music videos for Got7's "Stop Stop It" (2014) and Miss A's "Only You" (2015).<ref></ref>

### 2015–present: Sixteen, Twice and solo activities
*Sixteen* (TV program)|Twice}}

Chaeyoung participated in the reality television competition *Sixteen* in 2015.<ref name="Time" /><ref name="sixteen"></ref> Ranking sixth place out of the nine debut spots, she went on to join the newly formed girl group Twice as one of its <!-- Do NOT add MAIN, as it will be deleted as per consensus --> rappers and singers.<ref name="Time" /> On October 20, 2015, Twice officially debuted with the release of their first extended play (EP) *The Story Begins*.<ref></ref><ref name="ChaeOfTwice"></ref><ref name="Time"></ref><ref></ref>

Chaeyoung became the first member of the group to receive writing credits when she wrote a rap verse for Twice's version of J. Y. Park's "Precious Love" as part of their 2016 EP *Page Two.*<ref></ref> Since then, Chaeyoung has participated in writing lyrics for multiple songs by Twice. For the group's second Japanese studio album *&Twice*, on the track "How U Doin, Chaeyoung took part in composing for the first time, making her the first member to do so.<ref></ref> She also designed the limited edition cover of *Page Two*<ref></ref> and three different pairs of  shoes.<ref></ref>

As a soloist, Chaeyoung released covers of "Alone" by Cheeze,<ref name="ChaeOfTwice" /> "Off My Face" by Justin Bieber,<ref></ref> and "Weatherman" by Eddie Benjamin as part of the Melody Project series.<ref></ref> In 2022, she began learning to play the guitar in anticipation of a potential solo debut.<ref></ref> She then performed an unreleased solo track titled "My Guitar" on Twice's Ready to Be World Tour.<ref></ref> In October 2023, Chaeyoung collaborated with pop duo Coco & Clair Clair on a remix of their song "Pop Star".<ref></ref>

On June 10, 2025, JYP Entertainment announced that Chaeyoung was preparing for her solo debut.<ref></ref> Her debut studio album, *Lil Fantasy Vol. 1*, was released on September 12, with "Shoot (Firecracker)" serving as the lead single.<ref></ref><ref></ref> She also featured on rapper Sokodomo's single "Wake Up", released on November 20.<ref></ref> On November 21, JYP Entertainment announced that Chaeyoung had been diagnosed with vasovagal syncope and due to health concerns would be taking a hiatus until the end of the year.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

## Endorsements
In September 2023, Chaeyoung was selected as a brand ambassador for Italian fashion house Etro in Japan.<ref></ref> In November, she was chosen as the brand muse for Japanese cosmetics brand CipiCipi.<ref></ref>

## Personal life
Chaeyoung attended Hanlim Multi Art School with bandmate Tzuyu, graduating in 2019.<ref name="graduate"></ref><ref></ref> In 2020, Chaeyoung's personal phone number was leaked on social media; JYP Entertainment released a statement about the incident, and she responded directly on Twice's Instagram page.<ref></ref><ref>|language=ko|last=김|first=은애|date=January 26, 2020|access-date=May 1, 2020|archive-date=June 24, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200624152431/http://mosen.mt.co.kr/article/G1111298856|url-status=live}}</ref><ref></ref>

On April 5, 2024, JYP Entertainment confirmed that Chaeyoung is in a relationship with singer-songwriter Zion.T.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

She is Catholic and her baptismal name is Katarina.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

### T-shirt controversies
On March 18, 2023, Chaeyoung appeared wearing a shirt with imagery of QAnon, an American far-right conspiracy theory movement, during a promotion of "Set Me Free" on MBC's *Show! Music Core*.<ref></ref><ref></ref> The crop top showed a large Q in American-flag print with the words "We Go All" below it, a prominent slogan of the conspiracy theory group.<ref></ref> Three days later, on March 21, the singer posted a picture on Instagram in which she was wearing a T-shirt with Nazi imagery.<ref></ref> The T-shirt specifically featured an image of Sid Vicious, bassist of the Sex Pistols, wearing a tilted right-facing swastika identical to the one used by Nazi Germany.<ref></ref> Chaeyoung subsequently removed the Instagram post and issued an apology.<ref></ref><ref></ref> In the apology, she wrote, "I didn't correctly recognise the meaning of the tilted swastika in the t-shirt I wore."<ref></ref> Chaeyoung's label, JYP Entertainment, also issued an apology with regard to her Instagram post taking responsibility "for not thoroughly reviewing it from the label side."<ref></ref> The apologies by Chaeyoung and her label did not address the QAnon shirt.<ref></ref><ref></ref>
