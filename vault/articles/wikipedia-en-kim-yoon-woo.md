**Kim Yoon-woo** (; born December 28, 2000)<ref></ref> is a South Korean actor under Wells Entertainment.<ref></ref> He made his acting debut in the 2021 Netflix film *Sweet & Sour*. He is best known in *My Dearest* (2023) and *Begins ≠ Youth* (2024).

## Career
Kim made his acting debut in the 2021 Netflix film *Sweet & Sour*.<ref name="s&s"></ref>
