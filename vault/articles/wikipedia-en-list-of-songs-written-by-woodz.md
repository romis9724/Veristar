South Korean singer-songwriter, rapper and record producer Woodz has writing and production credits for over 115 songs. He has worked on songs for his own musical career, including songs recorded as a member of the South Korean–Chinese boy band Uniq and songs released under his former stage name and birth name Luizy and Cho Seung-youn respectively, as well as collaborations and works for other artists. Woodz has 95 works officially registered and credited by the Korea Music Copyright Association (KOMCA).<ref name="komca"></ref>
