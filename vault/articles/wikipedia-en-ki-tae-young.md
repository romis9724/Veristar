**Ki Tae-young** (; born **Kim Yong-woo** on December 9, 1978) is a South Korean actor. Ki made his acting debut in 1997, and went on to play leading roles in the television dramas *Creating Destiny* (2009), *Living in Style* (2011), and *Make Your Wish* (2014).<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> He is loved by viewers for starring on KBS 2 *Superman Returns* on January 24, 2016.<ref></ref> He also released an EP in 2012.<ref></ref> He married his *Creating Destiny* co-star Eugene on July 23, 2011.<ref></ref><ref></ref>
