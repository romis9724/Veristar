**Go Joon-hee** (; born August 31, 1985), birth name **Kim Eun-joo**, is a South Korean actress. She made her entertainment debut as a school uniform model in 2001, then launched an acting career in 2003.<ref></ref>

## Background
Kim adopted the stage name Go Joon-hee in 2008, after her character's name in *What's Up Fox* (2006).<ref></ref>

## Career
After playing supporting roles in *Girl Scout* (2008) and *Architecture 101* (2012),<ref></ref> she was cast as a lead actress in the 2011 indies *Drifting Away* and *Yeosu*,<ref></ref> the mainstream romantic comedies *Marriage Blue* (2013) and *Red Carpet* (2014),<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref> and the heist film *Intimate Enemies* (2015).<ref></ref>

Go has also appeared in several television dramas, notably *Listen to My Heart* (2011), *The Chaser* (2012), and *King of Ambition* (2013).<ref></ref><ref></ref> In 2013, she joined the fourth season of dating reality show *We Got Married* opposite Jinwoon of boyband 2AM.<ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref><ref></ref>

In 2015, Go starred in the hit romantic comedy series *She Was Pretty*.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

In February 2017, Go signed with YG Entertainment.<ref></ref> The same year, she starred in action melodrama *Untouchable*.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

In 2019, Go starred in OCN's supernatural thriller *Possessed*.<ref></ref><ref></ref>

In June 2019, Go signed with new agency C-JeS Studios.<ref></ref>

In March 2022, Go did not renew her contract with GLO Entertainment.<ref></ref> Later in May, Go signed a contract with Y-Bloom.<ref></ref>
