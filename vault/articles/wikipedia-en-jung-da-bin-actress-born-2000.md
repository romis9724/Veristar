**Jung Da-bin** (; born April 25, 2000) is a South Korean actress. She first gained recognition in 2003 as a commercial model for Baskin-Robbins and was widely dubbed as "Ice Cream Girl". In 2005, she made her acting debut through the television series *Wonderful Life*. Since then, she has appeared in several dramas including *She Was Pretty* (2015) and *The Flower in Prison* (2016).<ref name="Ref1"></ref> She transitioned to leading roles with *Extracurricular* (2020), *Live On* (2020–2021) and *High Cookie* (2023).<ref name="Extracurricular"></ref>

## Career
In April 2022, Jung Da-bin signed an exclusive contract with MAA.<ref></ref>

On October 14, 2025, it was reported that she signed an exclusive contract with Fantagio.<ref></ref>
